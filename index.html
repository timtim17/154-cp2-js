Time to Paint!

Your browser doesn't support HTML5 Canvas :( (really though Canvas support was added in Firefox 1.5, Chrome & Opera 9, and even IE 9... time to update your browser?)

Paint

Click and hold as you move around the canvas to paint.

Fill

Fills the entire canvas.

Rect

Draws a rectangle. Click once to set a corner, then click again somewhere else for another point.

Line

Draws a line. Click once to set one endpoint, then again to finish.

Freeform

Draws a freeform shape. Draw anywhere on the canvas like the Paint tool, and the shape you draw will be closed automatically.

Erase

"Draw" anywhere on the canvas like Paint, except you erase instead.

DelStroke

Click anywhere on the canvas, and the tool will erase the entirety of any stroke near where you click. Caution: a little temperamental on undo and redo.

Undo

Undoes the last stroke.

Redo

Redoes the last stroke.

Sticker

Allows you to add stickers to the Canvas.

Save

Saves your piece of art to a png file.
